Maintenance cleaning

Maintenance cleaning is all the work we do in a recurring period. Once a week until the end of the day as needed. You determine this cycle. Typical maintenance cleaning are hotels, restaurants, industrial companies, public areas etc.

Housekeeping

Do you have a stressful everyday life or job where cleaning is neglected?
